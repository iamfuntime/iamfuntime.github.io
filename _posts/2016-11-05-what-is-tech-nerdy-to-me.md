At this point, you may be asking yourself exactly what is Tech Nerdy to Me. Before we get into that, let's have some backstory. About eight months ago I was following something on Twitter and somebody posted something, I don't remember what at this point, bu tit was something about "talk nerdy to me", and I immediately had a lightbulb go off in my head. I should go purchase the domain [technerdytome.com][technerdytome]. Obviously I went and picked up the domain with absolutely no idea what I was going to do with it, so it sat there dormant. Fast forward to about a week ago when I decided it was time to put together a blog. I already knew where I was going to host it, but did I want my domain to be something bland or did I want it to point to a personal domain? When I decided I wanted it on a personal domain, it was time to find one that works. So I logged into [Gandi][gandi], my registrar of choice, and looked at what I had. That's when it happened, I saw [technerdytome.com][technerdytome] and immediately knew it was meant to be.

### Enough with the history lesson already

Alright, so at this point you're probably wondering exactly what this is going to be about? Well, the short answer is everything. People can nerd out about anything they are passionate about. I will write about the typical nerdy media (Star Wars, Star Trek, comic books, conventions, etc.) but I'll also write about things that pop up with work and infosec news. If I'm working on something kinda cool I'll post about it....at least what I can with those pesky NDAs in the way. I'll write about snowboarding and CrossFit. Anything that I feel that I "nerd out" on is fair game.

I guess the answer to the question is, this is my personal blog and I'm going to write about whatever I want.
